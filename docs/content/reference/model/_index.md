The game-world facts the engine simulates: entities, their attributes, and the
deterministic transforms over them. These pages are the **sole authority** the
engine and its tests build from. They are distilled from the draft
[rulebook](../../rules) by way of the resolved decisions in `GAME-DESIGN.md`;
only decided facts appear here.

A fact that lives in the rulebook or `GAME-DESIGN.md` but not in this section is
**not buildable** — promote it here first.
